This web application is designed to facilitate seamless interaction between students and teachers, with an admin overseeing the process. The key functionality of the platform is as follows:

## 1. User Roles

- **Student**: Can create requests for tutors and manage teacher requests.  
- **Teacher**: Can browse approved student requests and send their interest.  
- **Admin**: Approves student posts, monitors requests, and finalizes teacher assignments.  

## 2. Functional Flow

### Student Post Creation
- A student logs in and creates a **Tutor Request**.  
- The post initially remains **pending** until approved by the admin.  

### Admin Approval
- The admin reviews pending posts and approves or rejects them.  
- Approved posts are visible to teachers.  

### Teacher Requests
- Teachers log in and view approved student posts.  
- Interested teachers send a request to the student.  

### Student Selection & Payment
- The student reviews incoming teacher requests.  
- The student selects a teacher they prefer and makes a **payment** for the service.  

### Admin Final Assignment
- Once the payment is completed, the admin confirms and officially assigns the teacher to the student.  

## 3. Key Features

- **Role-Based Access Control**: Separate functionalities for students, teachers, and admin.  
- **Post Approval Workflow**: Admin approval ensures quality and security.  
- **Request & Selection System**: Students can choose their preferred teacher.
